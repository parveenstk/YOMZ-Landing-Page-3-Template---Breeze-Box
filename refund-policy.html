Refund Policy

At YOMZ, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations or arrive in the condition you anticipated. This Refund Policy outlines our guidelines for processing returns, issuing refunds, and handling exchange requests. By making a purchase from YOMZ, you agree to the terms below.

We offer refunds or replacements for eligible products returned within 7 days of delivery. To qualify for a refund, items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition in which they were received. Certain products, such as perishable goods, personalized items, digital downloads, or items purchased during clearance sales, are non-refundable unless they arrive damaged or defective. We reserve the right to decline refund requests that do not meet these eligibility criteria.

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incomplete, please contact our customer support team immediately, but no later than 48 hours after delivery. You must provide photographs or videos clearly showing the issue, along with your order details, to help us process your claim. In such cases, we will arrange for a replacement, repair, or refund depending on product availability and your preference. Refunds will be processed only after we verify the reported issue.

For returns due to reasons other than damage or defect—such as a change of mind, incorrect size, or unwanted gift—the customer is responsible for all return shipping costs. Products must be shipped back to us using a trackable method, and a tracking number must be shared with our support team. If the returned item is not received in its original condition, YOMZ may deduct a restocking or damage fee from the refund amount.

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 7–10 business days to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take additional time for the refund to reflect in your account. If your refund is rejected, we will provide an explanation and, where possible, offer alternative resolutions such as store credit or an exchange.

Please note that certain delays in refund processing are beyond our control, such as banking holidays, payment gateway issues, or international transaction timelines. While we always strive to process refunds promptly, YOMZ cannot be held liable for delays caused by third-party financial institutions.

For orders placed outside India, customs duties, taxes, and shipping charges paid by the customer are non-refundable. If a return is initiated from outside India, the customer is responsible for ensuring that the package is correctly declared and shipped to avoid additional customs penalties or delays. We recommend consulting your local customs office before sending an international return.
